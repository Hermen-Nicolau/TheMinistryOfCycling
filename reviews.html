This is a fairly new trail system with around 3.5 miles of open singletrack trail. The trail features some technical constructed features like ladder bridges and fast, bermed turns.

Salem Lake Trail

The Salem Lake Trail is a seven-mile loop on hard-packed doubletrack that follows the perimeter of Salem Lake. The trail connects to the Salem Creek Greenway which leads into downtown Winston-Salem.
